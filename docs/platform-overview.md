# Our Wave Community — Platform Overview

**Website:** [community.ourwave.org](https://community.ourwave.org)<br>
**Last Updated:** 2026-06-24

## Name

**Our Wave Community** — A trauma-informed, interactive online social media platform connecting survivors in a supportive, anonymous space for sharing, healing, and mutual support.

## Description

Our Wave Community is a fully realized social media platform where survivors of sexual harm, child abuse, and domestic violence can safely share their stories, post messages of support, engage in expert Q&A, and access curated resources—all within an accessible, trauma-informed digital space.

## Mission Statement

To create a compassionate, interactive community and digital space where survivorship is validated, healing is shared, and every story adds strength—bridging isolation with collective empathy and connection.

## Services & Features

- **Anonymous Storytelling** — Survivors can submit personal stories with anonymity and control, supported by secure processes, peer engagement, and thoughtful moderation.
- **Messages of Support & Hope** — A dedicated space for community users to leave anonymous encouragement and solidarity.
- **Expert Q&A Archive** — A growing library of expert-reviewed answers to survivor-submitted questions, promoting clarity and trusted guidance.
- **Curated Resource Referrals** — A broad, vetted set of resources (hotlines, therapy options, educational materials) catered for survivors and their allies.
- **Interactive Community Features** — A modern interface with commenting, enhanced accessibility, international support, speed optimizations, and trauma-informed design.
- **Community Engagement Tools** — Messaging of Hope, comment support, story reactions, and "milestones" marking community interactions.

## Highlights

- 555,331+ community users; 1,845+ stories; 1,719+ messages of hope; 813+ expert answers; 268,961+ resource referrals
- Community users across 51 U.S. states and 79 countries
- More than 1,816,292 content views
- Story submissions undergo sensitive review for anonymity, redaction, and trigger-warning application
- Platform guided by core values of being survivor-centered, protecting privacy and anonymity, and following trauma-informed principles
- Ensures anonymity, secure moderation, and trauma-informed design

## Core Values

- **Survivor-Centered** — Every decision, feature, and interaction is rooted in what serves survivors best.
- **Privacy and Anonymity** — Protecting the identities and safety of community members is non-negotiable.
- **Trauma-Informed** — All content moderation, communication, and community practices follow trauma-informed principles.
- **Inclusion** — The community welcomes survivors of all backgrounds, identities, and geographies.
- **Belief and Validation** — Survivors are heard, believed, and supported without judgment.
