# Our Wave — For Survivors

**Website:** [community.ourwave.org](https://community.ourwave.org)
**Last Updated:** 2026-06-24

## You Are Not Alone

Our Wave Community is a safe, anonymous space built specifically for survivors of sexual harm, child abuse, and domestic violence. Whether you want to share your story, read others' experiences, find resources, or simply know that someone understands — you belong here.

## What You Can Do on Our Wave

### Share Your Story

Share your experience anonymously through a secure, guided process. Every story is carefully reviewed to protect your identity and add appropriate content warnings. Your story can help others feel less alone.

**Get started:** [community.ourwave.org/share](https://community.ourwave.org/share)

### Read Survivor Stories

Browse stories from other survivors who have shared their experiences. These stories offer connection, validation, and the knowledge that healing is possible.

**Browse stories:** [community.ourwave.org](https://community.ourwave.org)

### Find Support Resources

Access a curated directory of vetted resources including crisis hotlines, therapy services, support groups, legal aid, and advocacy organizations.

**Find resources:** [community.ourwave.org/resources](https://community.ourwave.org/resources)

### Explore Expert Q&A

Read questions from other survivors answered by trauma-informed experts. Topics cover recovery, coping, relationships, legal questions, and more.

**Browse Q&A:** [community.ourwave.org/learn](https://community.ourwave.org/learn)

### Send a Message of Hope

Write an encouraging message for other community members. Your words of support can make a meaningful difference in someone's day.

## Your Safety Matters

- **Full Anonymity:** Your identity is never revealed. All stories undergo professional review for anonymity and redaction.
- **Content Warnings:** Trigger warnings are applied to all stories so you can make informed choices about what you read.
- **Secure Platform:** Our Wave uses trauma-informed design principles to create a safe digital environment.
- **Moderation:** All content is professionally reviewed before publication.
- **No Pressure:** Share as much or as little as you're comfortable with. Reading without sharing is completely valid.

## Crisis Resources

If you need immediate help in the United States:

- **National Sexual Assault Hotline (RAINN):** 1-800-656-4673
- **National Domestic Violence Hotline:** 1-800-799-7233
- **Crisis Text Line:** Text HOME to 741741
- **Childhelp National Child Abuse Hotline:** 1-800-422-4453
- **988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline:** Call or text 988

## Your Healing Journey

Healing is not linear. It looks different for everyone. Our Wave exists to support you wherever you are in your journey — whether you're just beginning to process your experience or you're further along and want to help others.

Every story shared adds strength. Every message of hope uplifts. Every question asked opens a door. You are part of a community that cares.
