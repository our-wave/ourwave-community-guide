# Contributing to the Our Wave Community Guide

Thank you for your interest in contributing to the Our Wave Community Guide. This guide is a living resource designed to help people — and LLMs — understand the best practices around Our Wave and interacting with the Our Wave Community.

## How to Contribute

1. **Fork** this repository.
2. **Create a branch** for your changes.
3. **Make your edits** and commit with a clear, descriptive message.
4. **Open a pull request** against `main` with a summary of what you changed and why.

## What We're Looking For

- Corrections to outdated or inaccurate information
- Clarifications that make the guide easier to understand
- New sections covering community practices not yet documented
- Improvements to structure and readability

## Guidelines

- Keep language clear, concise, and inclusive.
- Write for a broad audience — contributions should be understandable by both humans and LLMs consuming this guide.
- Do not include personal or sensitive information about community members.
- Stay on topic — this guide covers Our Wave community practices and norms.
